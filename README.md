# research
My personal research notebook used to document papers I read, ideas that occur to me, and resources I come across while exploring topics that interest me.
This repository is my personal research workspace.

My main interests currently include:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Robotics
- Computer Vision
- Neuroscience and Cognitive Science

That said, this notebook is intentionally open-ended. If something interesting crosses my path, it might end up here.

The goal is simple: learn deeply, ask questions, and keep a record of the journey.

---

## Structure

papers/  
Notes and summaries from research papers.

experiments/  
Small implementations, experiments, or prototypes inspired by ideas I'm studying.

resources/  
Interesting articles, lectures, tools, and references.

ideas/  
Rough thoughts, research directions, or questions worth exploring.
